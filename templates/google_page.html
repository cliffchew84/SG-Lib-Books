A library-centric view of your favourite Singapore library books!

Using the official NLB REST v2 APIs (29-11-2023), this site aims to provide an easier, single view of all your saved Singapore library books. Best viewed on desktop, but kinda mobile friendly. Read about this project Connect professionally

As of 10th Oct 2024, I switched to a new Google OAuth login. I will try to implement back this password login flow in the near future. Current users can port over to this new Google login flow by sending me your gmail through Linkedin, as I respond the fastest there!
